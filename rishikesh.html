üìç Overview

Rishikesh, the "Yoga Capital of the World," is a spiritual town situated on the banks of the mighty Ganges in Uttarakhand. Famous for its ancient temples, yoga ashrams, and the iconic Laxman Jhula, it offers a serene escape. But beyond spirituality, Rishikesh is also India's adventure hub, offering white-water rafting, bungee jumping, and camping by the river. Whether you seek peace or adrenaline, Rishikesh has it all.

üóìÔ∏è Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 Arrival & Ganga Aarti

Arrive in Rishikesh and check into your riverside hotel or camp. Visit the iconic Laxman Jhula and Ram Jhula. In the evening, witness the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti at Triveni Ghat or Parmarth Niketan, a spiritual experience filled with chanting and lamps.

Day 2 Adventure Day

Gear up for an adrenaline-pumping day with white-water rafting on the Ganges (Shivpuri to Rishikesh). You can also try cliff jumping or body surfing. In the evening, enjoy a bonfire with music at your camp or explore the local cafes.

Day 3 Spiritual Morning & Departure

Start your day with a rejuvenating yoga session by the river. Visit the famous Beatles Ashram (Chaurasi Kutia) and enjoy some healthy breakfast at a local cafe. Depart for your onward journey with refreshed mind and soul.
